# Research Data Management for Chemists

A [Carpentries Incubator](https://carpentries-incubator.org/) lesson on
research data management for PhD students and postdocs working in chemistry.

**Rendered lesson:** <https://aichemy-hub.github.io/rdm-for-chemists>

## Overview

This workshop (~3--3.5 hours including a break) covers:

- **Part 1 (Episodes 1--5):** General research data management principles --
  data management plans, FAIR data, storage, sharing, licensing, and funder
  policies.
- **Part 2 (Episodes 6--11):** Chemistry-specific data management --
  reproducibility in chemistry, electronic lab notebooks, metadata standards
  (InChI, JCAMP-DX, IUPAC FAIRSpec), chemistry repositories, managing
  analytical data (NMR, IR, MS), and the Physical Sciences Data Infrastructure
  (PSDI).
