## Priya — Second-Year PhD Student (Synthetic Chemistry)

Priya is in the second year of her PhD in synthetic organic chemistry. She
generates NMR, IR, and mass spectrometry data daily and currently stores
everything in a folder on her laptop labelled "PhD data". She has never
written a data management plan and is not sure what her funder requires.
She hopes to learn how to organise her data more systematically and
understand what she will need to do before submitting her thesis.

## James — Postdoc (Computational Chemistry)

James has just started a postdoc position, which will involve
running DFT calculations and molecular dynamics
simulations, generating large datasets on HPC clusters. He is aware of
FAIR data principles but is unsure which repositories to use for
computational chemistry data or how to write a good data access statement.
He is about to submit his first first-author paper from his PhD work and wants to get the
data sharing right.

## Aisha — First-Year PhD Student (Analytical / Materials Chemistry)

Aisha works at an interface between chemistry and materials science,
using a mix of techniques including XRD, electron microscopy, and
high-throughput screening. Someone in her group recently lost several weeks of processed
data when their laptop was stolen, so she wants to put a proper backup and
storage strategy in place. She is also curious about electronic lab
notebooks after her supervisor mentioned them.
